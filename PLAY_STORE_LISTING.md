## 🎯 STORE OPTIMIZATION TIPS

1. **Screenshots Strategy**: Show features, not just pretty pictures
2. **Description Clarity**: Use bullet points for scannability
3. **Keyword Placement**: Include main keywords in title and description
4. **Regular Updates**: Keep app up-to-date (shows active development)
5. **Respond to Reviews**: Build trust with your users
6. **Quality Over Quantity**: Better to have 4.5 stars than high volume

---

## 📊 AFTER LAUNCH MONITORING

Track these metrics:

- **Install growth**: Week-over-week comparison
- **Uninstall rate**: Keep below 30% 7-day retention
- **Rating**: Aim for 4.0+ stars
- **Review sentiment**: Monitor user feedback
- **Crash rate**: Keep below 1%
