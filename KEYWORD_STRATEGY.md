### Title/App Name

**Include:** Main keyword (BLE, ESP32, Controller)

```

**Include:** Secondary keyword + benefit

```

## ⚠️ KEYWORDS TO AVOID

❌ **Trademarked terms** (unless you have permission):

- Arduino (unless you officially support it)
- Amazon Alexa (unless integrated)
- Google Home (unless integrated)

❌ **Misleading keywords**:

- If your app can't do it, don't keyword stuff it

❌ **Irrelevant keywords**:

- Gaming, social media, etc. if not your focus

❌ **Competitor names** (unless truly comparing):

- Use "similar to" instead of copying competitor names
