## 🎨 VISUAL ASSETS FOR iOS

### A. APP ICON (1024x1024px)

**Requirements:**

- No rounded corners (iOS handles this)
- Safe zone: 956x956px center
- No transparency
- Flat design preferred
- Colors: Blue/tech themed

### B. PREVIEW IMAGES & SCREENSHOTS

**For iOS App Store:**

- Resolution: 1242x2208px (or 1170x2532px for newer iPhones)
- Minimum 2 screenshots, maximum 10
- Can include text overlays
- Should show your app's best features

**Screenshot Strategy:**

1. **Screenshot 1**: Main feature hook - Device discovery
2. **Screenshot 2**: LED RGB Controller
3. **Screenshot 3**: Vehicle Control Dashboard
4. **Screenshot 4**: Voice Commands
5. **Screenshot 5**: Connection Features

### C. APP PREVIEW VIDEO (optional but recommended)

- 15-30 seconds
- Shows quick walkthrough of main features
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Resolution: 1242x2208px or 1170x2532px
