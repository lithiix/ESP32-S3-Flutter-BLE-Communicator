## 📞 SUPPORT DURING LAUNCH

If you hit issues:

### App Store Rejection?

- Read rejection reason carefully
- Fix the issue (usually simple)
- Resubmit (usually approved within 1-2 hours)

### Low Ratings?

- Respond to negative reviews
- Ask "What can I improve?"
- Fix bugs and resubmit update

### No Downloads?

- Update screenshots (poor marketing)
- Improve description clarity
- Be patient (organic growth takes time)
- Consider modest marketing
